DPC Transportation Information

This page provides shuttle schedules, taxi services, and transportation resources for personnel arriving at the Deployment Processing Center.

✈ Frankfurt International Airport

  • Major arrival airport for international flights
  • Taxi or train available to Kaiserslautern
  • Travel time: 1–1.5 hours
