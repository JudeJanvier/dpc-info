Important Information for Personnel

Alcohol Policy

All military personnel assigned billeting at the DPC are prohibited from consuming alcohol for the duration of their stay. Possession of alcoholic beverages is also prohibited. If staff witness alcohol consumption, they will notify the Military Police and your Chain of Command. The PHA buildings are tobacco free. The only authorized smoking area is at or inside the gazebo. DPC Staff is available 24/7 at Building 233.
  • You must follow the instructions of the DPC staff.
  • Sleeping areas are assigned and males and females must remain separated.
  • If you leave the building, you must sign out and sign back in on the Break Roster at the front desk.
  • If you return due to flight cancellation or delay, you must sign back in at reception.
  • Headphones are required when using personal electronic devices.
  • You must clean any shared area after each use and clean your assigned bunk before departure.
  • Quiet hours (lights out) are from 2200 – 0600.
  • You must be properly dressed at all times.
  • Report any issues to the DPC staff at the reception desk in Building 235.

Facilities Information

  • Restrooms and showers are located in buildings 241 and 242.
  • Each building has separate sections for male and female use.
  • Laundry facility (free washers and dryers) is located at Building 268.
  • Laundry closes from 2100 – 0730 for maintenance.
  • The USO Center is open 24/7 at Building 235.
  • USO provides pool table, ping pong, TV's, snacks, games and coffee.
  • First aid kits are located in all buildings.
  • Fire extinguishers are located at all exits.
  • Rally point is located at Building 167 (Wash Rack).

Emergency Information

  • Report all accidents and incidents to DPC staff immediately.
  • Emergency DSN: 112
  • Emergency from cell: +49-6371-47-112
  • Stay calm and report your location (Rhine Ordnance Barracks building number).
