Welcome to the Deployment Processing Center

This portal provides important arrival information, facility guidance, transportation resources, and support services for personnel assigned to the DPC.

General Order #1 – Alcohol Policy
Alcohol is strictly prohibited for all personnel assigned to the DPC, regardless of branch of service. Alcohol consumption is not allowed on or off the installation during your stay. Possession of alcohol is also prohibited.

This policy follows General Order #1 signed by the Commanding General, 21st TSC.

If staff smell alcohol or witness consumption they will notify:
  • Military Police
  • Your Chain of Command

General Rules & Conduct

  • Follow all instructions given by DPC staff
  • Soldiers must be properly dressed at all times
  • Headphones required when using personal electronic devices
  • Clean shared areas after each use
  • Clean your assigned bunk before leaving
  • Quiet hours: 2200 – 0600

Billeting & Movement

  • Soldiers must sleep only in assigned areas
  • Male and female sleeping areas are separated
  • Sign out on the Break Roster when leaving
  • Sign back in when returning
  • If returning due to flight delay report to reception

Restrooms & Showers

  • PHA #1 (Building 231) restrooms located in Buildings 241 and 242
  • Other PHAs use container shower units
  • Use restrooms assigned to your gender listed in DEERS

Laundry Facility

  • Located at Building 268
  • Washers, dryers and detergent are free
  • Laundry closed 2100 – 0730

USO Center

  • Located in Building 235
  • Open 24/7
  • TV, Ping Pong, Pool Table
  • Games, Snacks and Coffee

Safety Information

  • First Aid Kits located in all buildings
  • Fire extinguishers located at exits and sleeping cubes
  • Emergency Rally Point: Building 167 – Wash Rack
  • Report all accidents or incidents to DPC staff

Smoking Policy

  • PHA buildings are tobacco free
  • No smoking within 50 feet of buildings
  • Smoking allowed only at the gazebo near the restrooms
  • Secure valuables at all times
  • Lockers available at Building 231
