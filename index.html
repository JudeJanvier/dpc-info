Welcome to the Deployment Processing Center

This portal provides important arrival information, facility guidance, transportation resources, and support services for personnel assigned to the DPC.

General Order #1 – Alcohol Policy
Alcohol is strictly prohibited for all personnel assigned to the DPC, regardless of branch of service. Consumption is not allowed on or off the installation during your stay. Possession of alcohol is also prohibited.

General Order #1 signed by the Commanding General, 21st TSC

If DPC staff detect the smell of alcohol or witness alcohol consumption, they are required to notify:
  • Military Police
  • Your Chain of Command

Information Center

Important guidance and facility information for all personnel staying at the Deployment Processing Center.

General Rules & Conduct

  • Follow all instructions given by DPC staff
  • You must be properly dressed at all times
  • Headphones required when using electronic devices
  • Clean shared areas after each use
  • Assigned bunk areas must be cleaned prior to departure
  • Quiet hours: 2200 – 0600

Billeting & Movement

  • Personnel must remain within assigned sleeping areas
  • Male and female personnel must remain in separate sleeping areas
  • Sign out on Break Roster when leaving
  • Sign back in when returning
  • Report flight delays to reception and Mayor cell

Restrooms & Showers

  • (PHA #1 – Building 231) Showers and restrooms located in Buildings 241 & 242
  • Separate sections for male and female personnel
  • Other PHAs Showers and restrooms located in container units in front of the buildings
  • Personnel must use the restroom corresponding to the gender listed in DEERS

Laundry Facility

  • Located at Building 268
  • Washers and dryers free of charge
  • Laundry detergent provided free of charge
  • Closed 2100 – 0730

🎮 USO Center

  • Located in Building 235
  • Open 24/7
  • TV, Ping Pong, Pool Table
  • Games, snacks and coffee

💊 Safety Information

  • First Aid Kits: Located inside all buildings and front areas of each PHA
  • Fire Extinguishers: Located at all exit doors and inside cube areas
  • Emergency Rally Point: Building 167
  • Personnel must immediately report any accidents or incidents to DPC staff or management.

🚭 Smoking Policy

  • All PHA buildings are tobacco-free.
  • Smoking, vaping, or the use of lighters is not permitted within 50 feet of any building.
  • Smoking allowed at gazebo
  • Secure valuables at all times
